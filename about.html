Welcome! Call me Sabina.

I'm currently working as a QA and UX Designer on Kindle in Motion, Amazon's recent ebook format innovation. We're pushing the bounds of traditional ebooks to give readers a better on-device experience and publishers more features to deliver their content. I strongly value thinking outside of the box, interdisciplinary collaboration, and iterative design (though there's something to be said about getting it "right" the first time!).

I've always known I wanted to create. But the what, where, and how changed drastically over time. What initially started as a photography and writing hobby led me to a passion to craft journeys that the user can not only see but evocatively experience - through touch, movement, sound, and more.
