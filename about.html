Sabina Cao
Seattle-area EX/UX/IxD designer and maker with constantly changing hair color

I'm currently working as a UX research and design consultant for Desinyx, a company I founded in 2017 to manage my professional side projects.

I strongly value thinking outside of the box, interdisciplinary collaboration, and iterative design (though there's something to be said about getting it "right" the first time!).

I've always known I wanted to create. But the what, where, and how changed drastically over time. What initially started as a photography and writing hobby led me to a passion to craft journeys that the user can not only see but evocatively experience - through touch, movement, sound, and more.

My current non-work obsessions:

  • Learning how to play this Adventure Time Medley on the piano
  • Clearing a corner of my backyard for a chicken coop
  • Planning a family trip to Vietnam
  • Cooking hearty soups and other Fall comfort foods
