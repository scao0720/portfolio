Overview

I started Desinyx in 2017 to manage the various side projects I am consulting or vending for. I provide a variety of UX design, research, QA, and creative design services currently, but I would like to expand that into experiential design, customer experiences, and overall strategy.

Clients

FCV Interactive

As the only researcher on the project, I have designed and am overseeing a full baseline and followup usability test for an FCV client. The test sessions are done remotely and are recorded for review. I'll be doing all the data analysis and reporting of actionable design recommendations, with input from supervising project managers and stakeholders.

The point of the work is to increase the usability and user understanding of the client's website, which will be shown by comparing the followup study results to the baseline.
